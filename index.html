Entity

Monad Origin is a non-disciplinary practice operating at the intersection of acoustics, material research, and object-making. Founded in 2022.

System

Sound recordings are processed to extract dominant frequencies. Each frequency becomes the structural basis for a unique physical artefact. No two translations share a source.

Lines

AF — Artefact. Autonomous objects. Editions of one.
RF — Resonant Furniture. Functional-sculptural works.
TL — Talisman. Small-scale frequency objects.

Access

Works are available to collectors, institutions, and galleries by direct dialogue. No public pricing. No open editions.
